Mega Mystery Mansion

  • Project Management
  • C#, Unity

The final computer science project that I convinced my friends to take to the next level. What was originally a 2 week project we started 5 months earlier, before working full time on it for a month and half. For the project I took the lead as project manager keeping our vision focused, and managing the overall design.

At it's heart it is a point and click adventure game about escaping a bizarre mansion that's home to talking rats, a sentient mannequin, and of course, cultists living in the basement. It's a weird game but I will forever value the lessons it taught me about game development, such as teamwork, how to lead a team, and how to plan effectively.
